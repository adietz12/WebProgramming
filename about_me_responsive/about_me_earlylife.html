I am a German-born U.S. political diplomat and foreign-policy adviser. I emigrated to the U.S. in 1938 and taught at Harvard for a little while. President Nixon then appointed me to assistant for national security affairs. Then from 1969 to 1975 I served as head of the national security council in the UN. I was eventually promoted to secretary of state from 1973 to 1977.
